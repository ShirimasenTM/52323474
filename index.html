  5. Adjustments to Flex vs Solo/Duo MMR ‚Äî if Solo/Duo MMR is higher than Flex, the system will bring Flex rank closer to Solo/Duo.

  7. New "Aegis of Valor": if you autofill and have mastery > C, you won't lose LP on a loss or you'll earn double LP for a win.

  9. Dodging a lobby will no longer allow players to escape autofill.
  10. Dodging will count as a full loss.
  11. Speeding up champ select by cutting timers and animations (~30 seconds faster).
  12. Reintroducing Duo Queue for higher ranks including Grandmaster and Challenger.
